# FeedbackLens single-message tests

Paste one message at a time in `Individual`.

Positive:
> The new dashboard is beautiful and very easy to understand. Exporting the report took two seconds.

Negative:
> I tried to upload my CSV three times and it failed without telling me why. This was frustrating.

Urgent:
> I was charged today but my account still says I have no access. Please fix this immediately.

Neutral:
> I would like to see a weekly email summary with the main trends and top customer complaints.

Good test flow:

1. Paste one message.
2. Click `Analizar con IA`.
3. Select the result card.
4. Generate or copy the draft reply.
5. Use filters: `Todos`, `Urgentes`, `Negativos`.
